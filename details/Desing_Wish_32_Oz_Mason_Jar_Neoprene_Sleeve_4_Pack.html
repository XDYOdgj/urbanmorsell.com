Discover the Perfect Mason Jar Accessory

The Desing Wish mason jar neoprene sleeve is designed for everyday use with your 32 oz jars. Its snug fit ensures a secure grip, making it easier to handle beverages, pickles, or other kitchen items without any hassle. The durability of stretchable neoprene fabric, combined with 4mm thickness, provides robust protection against damage and wear, helping your jars last longer and look better.

Thanks to the non-slip interior, your hands stay safe while you open or close jars. The insulated material also prevents condensation buildup, keeping your drinks at the right temperature for longer and avoiding slippery surfaces. Whether you're enjoying a homemade soda, fermenting your own preserves, or preparing for a gathering, these sleeves add both function and style to your routine.

Featuring a unique honeycomb embossing, these sleeves deliver a rustic, farmhouse aesthetic that complements any kitchen decor. They are suitable for both regular and wide mouth mason jars, offering a versatile solution for various uses. Plus, the sleeves are easy to clean, reusable, and come in a 4-pack with a range of color options to match your personal taste or event theme.
