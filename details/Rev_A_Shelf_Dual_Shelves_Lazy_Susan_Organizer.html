Rev-A-Shelf Dual Shelves Lazy Susan Organizer

Transform your kitchen storage with this stylish and practical dual shelves lazy susan organizer. Featuring a sleek kidney-shaped design, it allows for effortless access to your pots, pans, dishes, and kitchen gadgets, keeping everything within reach without the need to open cabinets.

This organizer is engineered with an adjustable telescoping shaft that can be customized from 26 to 32 inches. Whether you're working with a standard or custom cabinet setup, it adapts to fit your needs. The corner base cabinet compatible design ensures it works seamlessly in tight spaces, especially with 15-inch depth, maximizing your kitchen's storage efficiency.

Constructed from durable Maple wood and finished with a clear coat, this wooden storage rack not only looks great but also lasts for years. The independently rotating shelves can hold up to 64 pounds per square foot, giving you peace of mind when organizing heavy items like cookware and spices. With its modern and compact footprint, this spice rack is a must-have for small kitchens or those who love a clutter-free workspace.
